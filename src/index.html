Cineworld Ratings
A better way to find the best films at your local cinema

What?

Cineworld Ratings lets you view the movies airing at your local cinema. It adds some features over your Cinema's standard website; inline movie ratings, sorting/filtering by rating, mark films as watched.

Why?

I want to easily, at a glance, figure out which film to go see at the cinema. I can't do that with my cinema's website - I have to google/research each film individually.

What's next?

Currently only Cineworld cinemas are supported; I plan to add more. Beyond that, I want to receive notifications about upcoming high rated films.
